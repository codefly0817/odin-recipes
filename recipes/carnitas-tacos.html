Carnitas Tacos

A zesty Carnitas Taco that will make you go loco!

Lime juice in the crema give these carnitas tacos a good zing!

Ingredients

Carnitas:

  • 2 pound boneless pork shoulder roast
  • half cup chopped onion
  • 1/3 cup orange juice
  • 1 tbsp. cumin
  • 1 1/2 tbsp. kosher salt
  • 1 tbsp. dried oregano, crushed
  • 1/4 tsp. cayenne pepper

Lime Crema:

  • 1 lime, zested and juiced
  • 2 plain fat-free Greek yogurt
  • 1/2 kosher salt
  • 1 pinch chili powder (Optional)

Tacos:

  • 16 pcs. soft yellow corn tortillas
  • 4 leaves green cabbage, shredded
  • 1 cup very thinly sliced red onion
  • 1 cup pico de gallo (Optional)

Steps

  1. Make carnitas: Trim fat from pork. Cut pork into 2- to 3-inch pieces; transfer to a 4-quart slow cooker. Stir in onion, orange juice, cumin, salt, oregano, and cayenne.
  2. Cover and cook on Low 8 to 10 hours or High 4 to 5 hours.
  3. Transfer pork to a large bowl; shred using 2 forks. Add enough cooking liquid from slow cooker to moisten.
  4. Make lime crema: Add 2 tablespoons lime juice to a small bowl. Stir in yogurt and salt. Dust with chili powder.
  5. Make tacos: Serve pork in tortillas with shredded cabbage, red onion, and pico de gallo. Garnish with crema and lime zest.
